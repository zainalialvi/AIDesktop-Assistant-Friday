openAI response for Prompt: AI what is laptop 
************************


A laptop is a personal computer designed for portability, typically weighing 2-13 pounds and measuring less than an inch thick. Some laptops are even designed to be ultra thin and lightweight, making them easy to transport. Laptops typically feature a keyboard, a touchpad pointing device, and a display in a single unit. They also include ports used for connecting to external devices, such as a power cord, video connection, and USB ports.
